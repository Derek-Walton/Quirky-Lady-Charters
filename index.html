QUIRKY LADY CHARTERS

Bespoke Yacht Charters from Port Solent

Personalise your Yacht Charter

Step aboard and experience the stunning views of the South Coast in true comfort and style. Departing from Port Solent Marina, our bespoke yacht charter experiences offer everything from leisurely harbour cruises to exciting voyages across the Solent to the Isle of Wight.

Whether you're planning a special celebration, romantic getaway, or a relaxing day out with family and friends, we tailor every cruise to suit your needs. From scenic shopping trips to Gunwharf Quays, to anchoring in tranquil bays for a refreshing swim, your day on the water will be one to remember.

Our Signature Experiences

Our yacht charters are fully customisable and available as half-day or full-day packages. Some of our popular bespoke options include:

Afternoon Tea

Unwind with a luxurious Afternoon Tea.

Perfect for a relaxing day with friends, this experience combines classic British indulgence with a unique waterside setting. Let the conversation flow and the views drift by — no rush, no crowds, just good company in a beautiful setting.

Marriage proposals

Create a magical moment that will be remembered forever with a private marriage proposal.

Your proposal can be as relaxed or as elaborate as you wish. Whether timed with a golden sunset, accompanied by your favorite music, or paired with champagne on ice, we'll help you plan every detail to make it perfect. Let us help you make it unforgettable.

Wedding ceremonies

Celebrate your love with a truly unforgettable wedding ceremony.

Perfect for couples seeking a relaxed and private ceremony with close friends or family, we provide a beautifully set space onboard and a tailored experience to match your vision — from music and decorations to drinks and dining.

Overnight stays

Turn your day on the water into a truly unique getaway with an overnight stay.

Whether you're celebrating something special or just seeking a change of pace, your overnight stay includes a calm, stylish space to relax. Drift off to the gentle rhythm of the water and wake up to a new day in complete serenity.

Funeral wakes and ash scatterings

Say goodbye in a serene and meaningful way with a private farewell.

Our funeral wake and ash scattering cruises offer a calm, dignified setting to honour the life of a loved one. Whether you prefer a quiet moment with close family or a more personal celebration of life, we can tailor the experience to suit your wishes.

Corporate events

Host your next corporate event with a difference.

Whether you're entertaining clients, rewarding your team, launching a new product, or simply escaping the office for a fresh perspective, our yacht provides a unique and memorable setting. With panoramic views, a relaxed atmosphere, and tailored service, this is business — redefined.
